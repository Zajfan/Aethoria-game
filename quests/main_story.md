# Main Storyline of Aethoria

## Act I – Rising Shadows
Corruption spreads across the Elandor Plains. Villagers whisper of strange disappearances and shadowy figures. The player begins as a wanderer drawn into the conflict, uncovering the first signs of a greater evil.

**Key Quests:**
- Investigate missing villagers in the Whispering Marshes.
- Defend a Dawnwarden outpost from bandit raids.
- Discover fragments of an ancient prophecy hidden in Ashveil Peaks.

---

## Act II – Fractured Alliances
The factions of Aethoria begin to clash. The player must choose allegiances, shaping the balance of power. Trust is fragile, and betrayal lurks in every corner.

**Key Quests:**
- Negotiate peace between the Ironfang Clan and the Dawnwardens.
- Infiltrate the Guild of Whispers to uncover their plans.
- Aid the Circle of Verdant Flame in protecting sacred groves.

---

## Act III – Depths of Ashveil
The dwarven ruins beneath Ashveil Peaks reveal a prophecy foretelling the return of the Lich King. The player must descend into the depths, facing fire elementals and uncovering ancient secrets.

**Key Quests:**
- Explore the Ashveil Ruins and recover lost dwarven relics.
- Defeat the guardian drake of the volcanic caverns.
- Translate the prophecy with the help of Veilbound scholars.

---

## Act IV – The Lich King’s Return
The Crystal Wastes erupt with undead armies. The Lich King rises, threatening to plunge Aethoria into eternal night. Factions must unite—or fall divided.

**Key Quests:**
- Rally allies from across Aethoria to resist the undead.
- Defend the Shattered Coast from a naval invasion of spectral ships.
- Confront the Lich King’s generals in the frozen desert.

---

## Act V – Dawn or Dusk
The final choice: unite the factions to save Aethoria, or embrace shadow and reshape the world under the player’s rule. The outcome depends on past allegiances and decisions.

**Key Quests:**
- Lead the united armies in the final battle.
- Face the Lich King in his citadel of ice.
- Decide the fate of Aethoria: salvation or domination.
