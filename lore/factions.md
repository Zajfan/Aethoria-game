# Factions of Aethoria

## The Dawnwardens
Paladins sworn to protect the realm from corruption. They believe in unity and justice, but their rigid laws often clash with local customs.

## The Veilbound
Shadow mages who wield forbidden magic. Feared and mistrusted, they seek to reshape Aethoria through manipulation and secrecy.

## Ironfang Clan
Mercenary orcs who sell their strength to the highest bidder. Brutal but honorable, they respect strength above all else.

## Guild of Whispers
A network of spies, assassins, and smugglers. They control underground trade and thrive in chaos, profiting from war and instability.

## Circle of Verdant Flame
Druids devoted to balancing nature and civilization. They oppose unchecked expansion and fight to preserve the wild places of Aethoria.
