# Regions of Aethoria

## Elandor Plains
Rolling grasslands dotted with farming villages and trade routes. Bandits roam the roads, preying on merchants. The Plains are the breadbasket of Aethoria, but corruption has begun to seep into its soil.

## Ashveil Peaks
Volcanic mountains scarred by ancient eruptions. Fire elementals and drakes stalk the crags. Hidden within are dwarven ruins that whisper of a forgotten prophecy.

## Whispering Marshes
A fog-shrouded wetland where spirits linger and alchemists harvest rare herbs. Travelers often vanish here, lured by ghostly lights. The Marshes are a place of secrets and forbidden knowledge.

## Shattered Coast
Jagged cliffs battered by relentless waves. Pirates rule the strongholds here, and ancient sea temples lie half-submerged beneath the tides. The Coast is a frontier of danger and opportunity.

## Crystal Wastes
A frozen desert of shimmering ice and crystal spires. Once home to a thriving civilization, now ruled by the Lich King’s undead legions. Few dare to cross its glittering expanse.
